About Traveloge

Hi! I'm Sethum Dinusha, a Mechanical Engineering Technologist 
studying at Uva Wellassa University.
Beyond engineering, I'm passionate about exploring new places 
and documenting my travel adventures. From mountain peaks to 
hidden beaches, I love discovering the beauty of Sri Lanka 
and sharing these experiences.
This blog is my journey - a collection of travel stories, 
tips, and the incredible moments I've captured along the way.
Join me as I explore, learn, and grow through every adventure!

What You'll Find Here:

Travel guides and hidden gems in Sri Lanka.
Photography from my adventures.
Tips for fellow explorers.
 Stories from the road.

Whether you're planning your next trip or just love travel 
stories, I hope my experiences inspire your own adventures!
